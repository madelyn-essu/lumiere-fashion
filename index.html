Our Story

Founded in 2024 in the heart of the Philippines, LUMIÈRE represents the intersection of contemporary design and traditional craftsmanship. We partner with local artisans to create pieces that honor both heritage and modern aesthetics.

Each garment is produced in small batches using sustainably sourced materials, ensuring quality while minimizing environmental impact. From our atelier in Eastern Samar to your wardrobe, every piece tells a story of dedication and artistry.
